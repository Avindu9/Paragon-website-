The Ayurveda Paragon Hotel is about 130 km south of the Sri Lankan capital Colombo. The small town of Talpe, where we opened our Ayurveda spa hotel in 1996, can be reached from the airport in about two hours.

Light-flooded Ayurveda spa hotel under palm trees

Directly on the palm-lined beach of the Indian Ocean, a light and air-flooded house awaits you on arrival, Which will make you feel completely comfortable during your Ayurveda stay. It was built under the direction of a student of the renowned tropical architect Geoffrey Bawa. His typical construction of large, open areas and bright, friendly colors conveys transparency and space and creates a wonderfully tropical ambience. The furniture in our inviting foyer are authentic, antique collectibles that reflect the grand style of the past centuries.

Ayurveda under palm trees: Our spa hotel with its own herb garden and state-certified pharmacy is the perfect place for your Ayurveda cure in Sri Lanka, only a two hours' drive from the airport in Colombo and directly located at the Indian Ocean.
